  2. My favorite books.

    These four books offer a mix of inspiration, knowledge, and deep storytelling. "The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho takes readers on a magical journey of self-discovery, emphasizing the importance of following one's dreams. "Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" by Yuval Noah Harari provides a captivating look at human evolution, culture, and how we came to shape the world. "The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari" by Robin Sharma is a transformative tale about finding true happiness and purpose in life. Meanwhile, "To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee is a powerful novel that explores themes of morality, justice, and human empathy through the eyes of a young girl in the American South. Each of these books leaves a lasting impact and offers valuable lessons for readers.

    1. "The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho
    2. "Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind"
    3. "The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari"
    4. "To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee
